Summer of Solutions

With the social, political, and economic fallout of 2020 and incessant barrage of disheartening news, it is more important than ever to discuss solutions to our most pressing problems. For this reason, TEDxPlaceMuseux is working with scientists and community leaders to curate a three hour speaker series targetting the key issues of today.

This salon event is an opportunity to open a dialogue between our speakers and the audience by combining the power of a TEDx talk with the discourse of a vivid discussion. After the series of talks, the audience is invited to an open conversation to probe matters of economic recovery and systemic racism with select speakers.


Tune in August 15th at 17h00 CEST to catch the event hosted on Zoom. We hope to see you there!

What is TEDxPlaceMuseux?

At Sciences Po in the heart of Reims, the former Jesuit College today hosts almost 1600 young, forward-thinking, socially engaged learners. Independently organized by Sciences Po students, TEDxPlaceMuseux seeks to combine the spirit of learning and adaptation to an increasingly globalized world by featuring contemporary visionary speakers in this Age of Change. By exploring facets, opportunities, and engagement in a developing world, TEDxPlaceMuseux hopes to spark ideas, action, and hopefulness for current and prospective leaders. Join us on Saturday, March 27th, 2021 to take part in a remarkable conference that explores innovation and connection in this Age of Change.

TEDxPlaceMuseux is a student-run initiative that invites curiosity and engagement to the table to encourage discourse on multidimensional change. We seek to actively promote a diversity of perspectives to inspire intersectional solutions across the global community.
