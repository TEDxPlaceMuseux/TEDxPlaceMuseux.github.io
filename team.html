Meet the Team

Juliet Aze

Co-Organizer

Born in Baltimore Maryland, Juliet Aze grew up in the US and in France and is currently a student in the Dual BA program between Sciences Po Paris (campus of Reims) and Columbia University, where she studies social sciences. She is interested in applying what she studies in the spirit of “think globally, act locally.” She loves to write and is an avid reader. Her friends know her as the saver of bees, a lavender lover and the reference for all questions pertaining to Lord of the Rings.

Anna Bartoux

Co-Organizer

Anna Bartoux is a second year dual BA student at Sciences Po Paris and Columbia University. Born and raised in France, she later moved to the United States, where she developed a fascination for transatlantic relations and diplomacy. She hopes to focus her academic career on conflict resolution at the international level to later work in organizations such as the UN. As a political junkie and a history lover, she is also passionate about the dynamics of global change, so the theme “Age of Change” seemed perfect for this innovative conference. Anna is a self-diagnosed workaholic, and her friends sometimes joke that she spends way too much time on LinkedIn.

Carl Philip Dybwad

Co-Organizer

Carl Philip Dybwad is a circularity advocate, political campaign wonk, Creative Director of JUV Consulting, and a firm believer in visual communications ability to change the world. When not organizing for Elizabeth Warren’s presidential campaign, Carl is diving into the future of electioneering or creating geo-filters for Snapchat, where over 100 million people have used his designs. He is hoping to guide more business towards the future of green design, and hopefully make the world a bit more circular one step at the time

Pauline Bromberg

Head of Business

Pauline Bromberg is a second year in the Europe-Africa program at Sciences Po, majoring in Economics and Society. Raised in Silicon Valley in California, she has always been drawn to the power of ideas and their ability to bring about meaningful social change. As head of sponsorships, Pauline hopes to bring the local rémois community and sciences pistes together, allowing them to engage and learn from one another. In her free time, Pauline can be found dragging her friends on never-ending hikes, reading incessantly, or taking advantage of all of the great bread France has to offer.
