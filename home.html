What is TEDxPlaceMuseux?

At Sciences Po in the heart of Reims, the former Jesuit College today hosts almost 1600 young, forward-thinking, socially engaged learners. Independently organized by Sciences Po students, TEDxPlaceMuseux seeks to combine the spirit of learning and adaptation to an increasingly globalized world by featuring contemporary visionary speakers in this Age of Change. By exploring facets, opportunities, and engagement in a developing world, TEDxPlaceMuseux hopes to spark ideas, action, and hopefulness for current and prospective leaders. Join us on February 27th, 2021 to take part in a remarkable conference that explores innovation and connection in this Age of Change.

TEDxPlaceMuseux is a student-run initiative that invites curiosity and engagement to the table to encourage discourse on multidimensional change. We seek to actively promote a diversity of perspectives to inspire intersectional solutions across the global community.
