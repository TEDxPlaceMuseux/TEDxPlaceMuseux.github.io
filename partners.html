Becoming a Partner

TEDxPlaceMuseux is an annual conference organized by Sciences Po students dedicated to the spread and discussion of ideas through innovative and thought-provoking talks. TEDxPlaceMuseux invites curiosity and engagement by encouraging discourse on the meaning of change while actively promoting a diversity of perspectives to inspire intersectional solutions across the global community.

In order to curate such a forward-thinking conference, we are looking for like-minded partners who can help us assure the high quality and low cost of the event. Partnering with us can prove to be mutually beneficial as partners gain greater exposure with a conference that reaches people from our community and around the world and offers collaboration with the changemakers that are driving innovation in every sector.

Benefits of becoming a partner with TEDxPlaceMuseux include:

  • VIP passes to TEDxPlaceMuseux with reserved seating
  • Publicity on campus and with the Reims community
  • Exclusive access to Discovery Session: a moderated discussion among partners about their work surrounding the Age of Change
  • Recognition in the event program and on conference slides*
  • Recognition online and at the venue
  • Network with speakers at the VIP reception

Please email tedxplacemuseux@gmail.com to inquire about how to become a sponsor.

*TEDx Talks are recorded educational content often used in classrooms. For this reason, partners with ties to alcohol are not allowed to be represented on conference slides.
